Latitude vs. Max Temperature

The significance of this scatterplot is showing that clearly Latitude has an effect on Max Temp. For this specific day (1/15/17), we see that cities that are around the equator on average are showing the highest temperatures, and as we move further away from the equator towards both poles that average temperatures start to decline. Thus, showing the bell like curve in the visual.
