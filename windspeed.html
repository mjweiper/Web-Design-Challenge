Latitude vs. Wind Speed

This plot shows us city latitudes relative to the percent wind speed for 1/5/17. Looking over the plot I believe there is some significance between the two as it seems that the higher wind speeds are close to the south pole. Once again I am sure there are other factors to this, but I feel like we can make a good case latitude is a primary trigger for wind speed.
